- `concepts/turboquant-kv-cache-compression.md` — KV cache quantization (asymmetric Q8-K + Turbo-V), 6x compression, enables 131K on 16GB Mac Mini

**Key findings:**
- Symmetric TurboQuant degrades quality severely (needle-in-haystack: 1/3 at 32K context)

- M4 Mac Mini: compute-bound, no speed benefit but massive context headroom gain
- Qwen 3.5 models respond best on Apple Silicon
- llama.cpp mainline not yet merged; TurboQuant Plus fork available
