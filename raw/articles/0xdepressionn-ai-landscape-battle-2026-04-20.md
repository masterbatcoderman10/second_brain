## 3 / 3 | Running an RP server: the subscription business Rockstar built for you

The intro mentioned someone with 200 paying members at $20/month.

That's $4,000/month. Recurring. Without shipping a single product update.

FiveM RP servers are subscription businesses. The best ones run whitelist systems - players apply, get approved, and pay a monthly membership fee to keep their slot. Server owner sets the economy. Server owner sets the rules. Server owner collects the payments through Tebex while the community runs itself.

This isn't streaming. This isn't selling a one-time product. This is closer to running a private club where the members pay to stay in.

The biggest FiveM RP servers have waitlists. Hundreds of people waiting to pay for access. Because the experience is that good - custom economy, custom jobs, 50-100 other players in a living world that feels nothing like the base game.

The barrier that kept people out: you needed Lua to build the server, and you needed Lua to customize it. Same barrier as before.
