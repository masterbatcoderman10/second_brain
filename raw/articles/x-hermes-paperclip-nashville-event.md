## Claims about Hermes
- **Lineage:** Described as "evolution of Openclaw". (Analogy: Openclaw = Charmander, Hermes = Charizard).
- **Key Features:** Self-learning, persistent memory, fewer security issues.
- **Deployment:** Local machine or $24/mo Digital Ocean droplet.
- **Capabilities:**
  - "Writes like you" after 10-12 examples (feedback loop).
  - Manages email support (remembers customers).
  - Manages community (answers questions, remembers member projects).
- **Interface:** CLI.

## Claims about Paperclip
- **Type:** Orchestrator (not an agent).
- **Analogy:** Hermes = star employee, Paperclip = CEO.
- **Function:** Assigns work to Hermes (or other models).
- **User Role:** "Board of Directors" (approves/denies budget and plans).
- **Example Usage:** Marketing agency (SEO, social media, Google ads) for multiple brands.
- **Communication:** Sends text messages for approval/denial.
- **API:** Has an API (allows Hermes to eventually replace the human "Board").
