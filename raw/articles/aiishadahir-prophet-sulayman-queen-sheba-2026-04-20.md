“Who will bring me her throne before they come to me in submission?” 27:38

He was speaking of the Queen of Sheba & wanted the throne brought to his court instantly. A powerful Jinn offered to bring it before he could stand up. But then, a human stepped in.

This man didn't use muscles or magic. The Quran says he had "Knowledge of the Book."
In the time it took for Sulaiman to blink, the throne was there.
