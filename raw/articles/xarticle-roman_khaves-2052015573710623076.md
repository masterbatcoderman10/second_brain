## II. AI slideshows

the easiest format on tiktok right now.

slideshows pull 70%+ completion rates vs 40% for video. the algorithm rewards completion above almost everything. each swipe is a micro-commitment.

that's why there are a lot of ai slideshow campaigns on affiliatenetwork.com.

formats that convert for peptides:

> before/after transformation:
"day 1 vs day 6 on reta." slide 1 = bad state with negative labels. slide 2 = improved state with positive labels. same angle, same outfit.

> metric progression:
"30 days on retatrutide." each slide shows a different metric (weight, waist, appetite, mood) trending in the right direction.

> problem/solution carousels:
"things to avoid on reta." slide 1 names the mistake. slides 2 through 6 give the fix. final slide introduces the brand.

> list breakdowns:
"best peptides to glow up in 2026." one slide per peptide. one image plus 2 to 3 benefits.

> contrarian curiosity hooks:
"why i'm not taking peptides." slide 1 rejects the trend. slide 2 gives a weak reason. creates demand by performing skepticism.

> ironic reverse lists:
"peptides are not worth it." each slide lists benefits framed as warnings. humor disguises the pitch.

> assumed adoption:
"what to do on reta." skips the question of whether you should take it. frames usage as already normal.

> lifestyle proof:
gym shots, meals, physiques layered between text slides. credibility without explicitly proving anything.
