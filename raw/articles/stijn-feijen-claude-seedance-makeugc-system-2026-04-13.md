## Step 1 — Choose a Product That Can Sell

Pick something that:

- Solves a clear problem

- Has visual appeal (can be shown in action)

- Fits short-form content

Examples:

- Skincare

- Fitness tools

- Pet products

- “Problem-solving” gadgets

If it wouldn’t work on TikTok, skip it.

## Step 2 — Generate Content Angles with Claude

Use Claude to eliminate guesswork.

Prompt:

“Give me 20 high-converting TikTok ad angles for [product], focused on pain points, transformations, and curiosity hooks.”

You’ll get:

- Hooks

- Concepts

- Emotional triggers

Select the best 5–10 to start.

## Step 3 — Turn Angles into Scripts

Ask Claude:

“Write 10 short 15-second UGC-style scripts with strong hooks and clear CTAs.”

Now you have:

- Ready-to-use scripts

- Scroll-stopping openings

- Conversion-focused structure

Now you activate the testing engine.

Spend ~45 minutes inside MakeUGC reviewing:

- Watch time

- Conversions
