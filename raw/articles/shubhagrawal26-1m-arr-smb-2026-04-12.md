## Raw Tweet Text

1m arr is 83k per month - if you're just starting out and still building a skill, you'll likely only be able to get SMBs as customers

If you can build something that costs $350/mo, you will need 237 people to pay you this amount every single month.

(Either a SaaS or a productized micro-service with specific results delivered monthly without any input from the business ur selling to)

SMBs churn within 3-5 months when you ask them to pay $2-5k a month, while they retain for 20-30 months at a sub $400 price point with a clear value.

Build a service with 80-90% gross margins that costs the client $350 a month, build a no-brainer offer around some guarantee with some qualification needed to avail it

Aim to close 40 deals a month, at a 30% close rate - that's 120 calls booked every single month.

You'll be at 1m arr in the 6 months.

I haven't accounted for any churn, but haven't accounted for any expansion either.

the hard part is
-> knowing what to sell,
-> maintaing said gross margins,
-> providing a predictable/repeatable value,
-> crafting a no-brainer offer around it
-> booking initial calls

but if you're able to solve this once - these companies are a LOT EASIER to scale to 10, 20 or even 30mil a year

if you go the high ticket services route ($3k to $5 a month) - they churn within 3-4 months if SMBs

which is why most agencies don't cross $3m and the $3m to $10m journey is like eating glass - CAC rises and margins shrink

Only solution - go back to this lower ticket model and move upmarket (difficult if ur a newbie)
