# INSID3: Training-Free In-Context Segmentation with DINOv3

Training-free, in-context video and image segmentation using DINOv3 features.

## Overview

INSID3 leverages DINOv3's visual representations for zero-shot, training-free segmentation. Given a small number of example segmentations (in-context), it segments new images or video frames without any fine-tuning or additional training. Marked as a highlighted paper in the CVPR 2026 list.
