# AI Implementation Consulting

## Definition

A premium freelance service where a solo operator or small team works with 10-50 person SMBs to audit their operations, design AI/automation systems that eliminate repetitive manual work, build those systems, and train the team to run them — then stay on retainer for ongoing tuning and expansion.

**Why it's called "the $12K/month service":** A fully-loaded monthly retainer at $12,000/month on a 6-month minimum is the target model. At $75/hour blended labor rate and a 20-person team recovering 15 hours/week, the ROI for clients is $18-25K/month in recovered labor — making $12K a trivial math problem.

## Why AI Can't Replace This Service

AI builds the workflows. It does not pick which workflows to build.

**The human layer requires:**
- Understanding the specific business (talking to ops lead, watching sales team fumble between five tools)
- Identifying where the actual bottleneck sits (e.g., the 3-day gap between a demo and a follow-up)
- Accountability when the system breaks at 9pm before a launch
- Human judgment on which problems to solve first

## The Three Offer Structure

| Offer | Price | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Audit only | $3,000 flat / 1 week | 30-page prioritized playbook; 20% convert to full |
| Build only | $15,000 fixed / 6 weeks | 3-5 working systems, no retainer |
| Full engagement | $12,000/month minimum | Audit + build + train + ongoing tuning |

**The audit is the Trojan horse** — gets operators inside the business. The retainer is where the revenue lives.

## How to Land First Clients

1. Use existing network — every founder you know is buried in tools and losing hours
2. Offer audit at $1,500 (discounted from $3,000) in exchange for a case study
3. Turn case study into LinkedIn post (specific numbers) + 10-minute before/after video
4. Post weekly, DM every 10-50 person business owner who engages
5. Lead with a question — "Where are you losing time?" — not a pitch
6. Repeat: 3 conversations → 1 discovery call → 1 audit → full engagement

## Tool Stack for Solo Operators

- **Automation:** n8n + Make
- **Context systems:** So AI actually knows each client's business without re-explaining
- **Prompt engineering:** For fast context building from client conversations

**Without context systems:** The service runs you (AI asks the same questions every time).
**With context systems:** You run the service.

## Why August Is the Deadline

- Every month, another wave of businesses realizes their AI purchases didn't fix anything — demand curve bending up
- Every month, more operators figure out this service exists — competition filling in
- Four months is enough to build the stack, land first two clients, have revenue before the field gets crowded
