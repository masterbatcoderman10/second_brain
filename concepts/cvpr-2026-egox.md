# EgoX: Egocentric Video Generation from a Single Exocentric Video

Generative model that converts an exocentric (third-person) video into an egocentric (first-person) view.

## Overview

EgoX takes a single third-person video and synthesizes what the first-person view would look like, enabling conversion between camera perspectives. Useful for robotics, VR content generation, and sports analysis.
