# LLM SEO

## Definition

LLM SEO is the practice of shaping pages and site structure so they rank not just in traditional search, but also in LLM-driven discovery layers such as ChatGPT and other AI answer surfaces. In [[denohawari]]'s framing, it is now a distribution channel for the highest-intent users rather than a side effect of classic SEO.

## Core claim from the source

According to [[denohawari]], LLM-originated traffic converts at 18%, higher than any other traffic source in the cited study. The thread argues that brands over-invest in outreach, paid ads, and cold email while under-investing in visibility for AI-mediated search behavior.

## Operating playbook

The source highlights five working components:

1. Capture high-intent "AI study tool" keywords.
2. Use competitor displacement to win 5K+ daily clicks.
3. Rebuild pages for depth and intent alignment.
4. Build backlinks to move domain rating from 5 to 21.
5. Layer traditional [[programmatic-seo]] with LLM visibility tactics for ChatGPT-facing discovery.

## Reported outcomes

- Client revenue: $20K MRR → $100K+ MRR in 4 months
- Revenue growth: 5x
- SEO contribution: 95%+ of organic traffic
- Search Console screenshot in the source shows 171K clicks, 1.25M impressions, 13.7% CTR, and 12.4 average position

## Why it matters

This positions LLM SEO as an extension of [[programmatic-seo]], not a replacement for it. The important shift is channel definition: instead of only chasing blue-link rankings, pages are optimized for citation, retrieval, and recommendation inside AI interfaces. That makes information gain, intent coverage, and comparative clarity more valuable than generic SEO volume plays.
