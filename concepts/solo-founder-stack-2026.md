# Solo Founder Stack 2026

The team chart for a $1M solo founder in 2026 — five jobs, one person, zero hires.

## The Engineer: Claude Code

Three-step setup:
1. **CLAUDE.md** - conventions file at repo root (like tech lead day-one for senior hire)
2. **Skills** - markdown files teaching agent how to do one class of task end-to-end (ship-feature.md, triage-bug.md, migrate-schema.md)
3. **MCP integrations** - GitHub, Postgres, Slack, monitoring, Linear, filesystem wired once and used forever

"Pipe prompts into the chat without the setup and you have an autocomplete. Do the setup and you have a hire."

## Back Office: Five Pipelines

Built in a weekend on the same Claude Code setup:
1. **Video repurposing** - YouTube link → 10 platform-ready posts
2. **Lead enrichment** - CRM lead → first-touch email by coffee-brewing time
3. **Competitive intelligence** - 10 competitor sites every Monday → brief
4. **Invoice extraction** - PDF → clean Xero records
5. **Knowledge base agent** - drafts support replies in your voice with citation

## Research Layer: Hermes Agent or OpenClaw

- **Hermes Agent** (Nous Research) - cloud research, scheduled cron jobs, subagents in parallel, persistent memory, programmatic tool calling
- **OpenClaw** - local-first, reads Obsidian vault, Notion exports, local PDFs, terminal output

Three research jobs to set up first:
1. Scrape top 50 ads in category from Meta Ads Library every Monday → brief on Telegram
2. Monitor X + Reddit for posts with 5K+ impressions in niche → hook candidates
3. Read support tickets weekly → surface top 3 customer pain phrases

## Marketing: Higgsfield Marketing Studio

One paste, one persona, one marketing department. Hermes Agent scrapes Meta Ads Library + TikTok Creative Center, writes brief, Seedance 2.0 renders videos. 500+ ad-ready cuts per day in 9:16, 1:1, 16:9.

## The AI Persona Play

Aitana López (fitness model, Barcelona, ~$11K/month brand deals) and Lil Miquela (since 2016, 8-figure annual) prove the model. Higgsfield generates locked face + voice + vibe persona.

## Key Insight

"The product was fine. The product was sometimes excellent. Nobody saw it." Marketing was the job that didn't get automated when building did. Now it has been.

The companies hitting $1M solo over the next two years will be the ones who figured out the team chart first — not the ones with the best models or most credits.
