# Behavioral Science UGC

Applying cognitive and social psychology principles systematically to AI UGC script writing and content production, so that each structural element of a reel activates specific biases known to drive viewer attention, emotional engagement, and conversion.

## Core Thesis

Viral UGC is not born from intuition or "good创意" — it is engineered by stacking cognitive biases at each stage of the viewer journey. Operators who internalize this stack and embed it into their production system produce content that is systematically more likely to stop the scroll, hold attention, create emotional engagement, and drive conversion.

## The Bias Stack (per [[adrian-solarz]])

### Stage 1: First Frame
- **Novelty Bias** — brain allocates disproportionate attention to meaningfully different stimuli
- **Orienting Response** — involuntary attention redirect toward unexpected stimulus (micro-imperfection in first 3 seconds)
- **Contrast Effect** — perceptual contrast catches eye

### Stage 2: Hook
- **Information Gap Theory** — curiosity = discomfort from gap between known and wanted; keeps viewer watching
- **FOMO** — framing offer as something a defined peer group is already accessing (not abstract possibility)

### Stage 3: Pain Section
- **Empathy Gap** — present-tense experiential language induces mild version of the pain state
- **Loss Aversion** — concrete ongoing cost of inaction activates loss motivation (2x stronger than gain framing)
- **In-Group Bias** — persona-specific language signals "this person gets my exact situation"

### Stage 4: Mechanism Bridge
- **Zeigarnik Effect** — open loops at transitions carry viewer through with active motivation
- **Anchoring Effect** — mention failed alternative costs before product price; absolute value appears smaller

### Stage 5: Transformation
- **Narrative Transportation** — deep engagement suppresses counterarguing; exact persona language drives it
- **Awe Response** — makes viewer imagine the outcome from inside the narrative

### Stage 6: CTA
- **Reciprocity** — how-to content delivered before any ask; viewer feels obligated
- **Commitment & Consistency** — quiz funnel builds small commitments; rejecting recommendation = rejecting self

### Stage 7: Comments
- **Social Proof Cascade** — pinned comment confirms others like the viewer found it worth it

## Key Production Tactics
- **Monthly novelty audit:** top 30 reels in niche — use visual elements appearing in fewer than 3
- **1 imperfection in first 3 seconds:** subtle enough not to read as error, clear enough to trigger orienting response
- **Present-tense pain language:** "I am exhausted" not "I was exhausted"
- **Exact persona language:** copy from customer reviews verbatim, don't paraphrase
- **Zeigarnik loops:** 2–3 per script at major section transitions

## Campaign Results
- Skincare client reel: **3.1M views in 4 days** using bias-mapped script
- Total client revenue through viral reels: **$800k+**
