# User Acquisition

Processes and channels for acquiring new users or customers. A core startup concern post-PMF.

## Channels

- **Outbound**: direct outreach, cold email, LinkedIn, sales
- **Inbound**: content marketing, SEO, viral loops, organic social
- **Paid**: paid ads, affiliate programs, marketplace placements

## Key Concepts

- Activation funnels
- Referral loops
- Channel-market fit
