# Costco Algorithm

## Overview

Nick Sleep's term (documented in *"The Psychology of Money"* by Morgan Housel) for **Economies of Scale Shared** — companies obsessed with delivering more value to end customers per dollar spent.

## Core Mechanism

The flywheel:
1. Company delivers exceptional value-per-dollar
2. Goodwill compounds → more capital
3. Lower prices → more customers
4. More customers → more capital → repeat

## Relevance to [[ontology-velocity-investment-framework]]

The Costco Algorithm is one component of Antonio Linares's investment framework — specifically the "value compounding" leg. Companies executing the Costco Algorithm build pricing power and customer loyalty that compounds over time, creating a sustainable competitive moat.
