# Ecommerce Funnel Training

Ecommerce Funnel Training is the six-stage subscription ecommerce playbook used by billion-dollar DTC brands (AG1, Grüns, IM8 Health). The framework covers: (1) value-stacked acquisition offers available only for subscription, (2) massive Meta ad volume with AI statics + influencer collab videos, (3) custom pain-point landing pages (gut-health, science, feel-again, why-im8, competitive) aligned to each ad's targeting, (4) quiz-based email pop-ups that surface the existing offer without extra discounting, (5) aggressive early email/SMS flows to convert before competitor retargeting, and (6) post-purchase education-first retention that shifts from selling to keeping subscribers engaged. The key insight is that most brands leak customers at every stage post-click; the funnel architecture ensures each stage connects to the next with zero wasted acquisition spend.

## Funnel Architecture

### Stage 1: The Offer
Subscription-only value-stacked deals (e.g., 30% OFF + $72 in gifts for IM8; vs one-time barely visible and $34 more). The subscription LTV at 6 months is 50–100% of a one-time purchaser's LTV, and 2x at 12 months.

### Stage 2: Ads
1,000–2,500+ live Meta ads at once. 50/50 static (AI-generated educational infographics + direct offer) and video (UGC + influencer white-label/collab ads). Kill losers weekly; newness drives down CPMs.

### Stage 3: Custom Landing Pages
10–20 pain-point-specific pages per brand. Examples from IM8: gut-health (listicle about gut issues), science (clinical/ingredient angle), feel-again (energy), why-im8 (convenience), competitive (athletic performance). Every page drives to the same acquisition offer.

### Stage 4: Pop-Up Forms
Fired 2–4 seconds post-load. Ask a question first (commitment bias), then request email. Highlights the existing site-wide offer rather than an extra discount—zero marginal cost for email capture. Zero-party data collected via the quiz answer.

### Stage 5: Email & SMS Flows
Daily emails and SMS in the first days post-capture. Infographic-forward design, offer at top of every email, founder text-style emails that feel personal and stand out in inbox. Aggressive early because post-signup Meta feeds competitor ads—convert before they go cold.

### Stage 6: Post-Purchase Retention
Shift entirely to education once subscribed. Remind to use product, reaffirm the decision, show benefits. Email frequency drops after purchase—every email to a subscriber is a cancellation reminder if it screams "subscription exists."

## Key Insights

- The funnel is the real differentiator between $100M brands and everyone else—not ads, not product
- Most brands leak post-click; the architecture connects every stage so cold traffic converts on first visit
- Email capture via pop-ups shouldn't cost extra margin—just surface the existing offer
- Retention = educating, not selling; let the product do the heavy lifting
