# Claude Fitness Prompts

Seven detailed [[claude-code]] prompts by [[hawks0x]] for fitness programming, published as a viral X Article (March 26, 2026, 2,332 likes).

The premise: a personalised 12-week training programme from a personal trainer would cost hundreds of pounds; these prompts replicate that output in 10 minutes via Claude for free.

## The 7 Prompts

| # | Prompt | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | **The ultimate training programme** | 12-week periodised hypertrophy + strength programme tailored to exact stats, schedule, equipment |
| 2 | **The plateau** | Diagnose why specific lifts have stalled; 8-week fix across programming, recovery, nutrition, psychology |
| 3 | **The missing link** | Identify lagging muscle groups and performance weak points; prioritised corrective action plan |
| 4 | **The recovery protocol** | Sleep optimisation, mobility routine, nervous system downregulation, deload strategy, supplementation |
| 5 | **The injury prevention blueprint** | Prehab routine for compound lifts; warm-up protocol; return-to-training after injury |
| 6 | **Progress tracker** | Weekly check-in template, photo/measurement protocol, data-driven decision-making guide, monthly review |
| 7 | **Body recomp strategy** | 16-week muscle-building + fat-loss simultaneous programme; training, nutrition, cardio, tracking |

## Pattern

All prompts follow a consistent structure: role assignment → personal stats input → specific output requirements → format directive. The persona is always an elite specialist (strength coach, physiotherapist, sports nutritionist). Fill-in bracketed fields personalise the output.
