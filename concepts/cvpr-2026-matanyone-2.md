# MatAnyone 2: Scaling Video Matting via a Learned Quality Evaluator

Video matting system that scales to long videos using a learned quality evaluator to guide inference.

## Overview

MatAnyone 2 addresses video matting at scale by introducing a learned quality evaluator that assesses output quality per frame, enabling efficient allocation of computation across long video sequences. Builds on the original MatAnyone approach.
