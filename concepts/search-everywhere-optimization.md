# Search Everywhere Optimization

SEO framework coined/refined by [[nathangotch]] — rebranding SEO as **Search Everywhere Optimization** rather than fragmented sub-disciplines.

## Core Thesis

Rather than chasing new acronyms (AI SEO, GEO, AEO), optimize for the underlying principle: being found everywhere buyers search.

## Component Layers

| Layer | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Local pack** | Google local results for commercial queries; undervalued vs. blog content |
| **AI Overview / AI Mode** | Google's AI-powered answer surfaces; ranking patterns mirror local pack |
| **GEO** | Generative Engine Optimization — content optimized for AI answer extraction |
| **AEO** | Answer Engine Optimization — structured content designed to be extracted as direct answers |

## Key Claim

Strong performance in Google's local pack for commercial queries correlates with strong visibility in AI Mode, AI Overview, and Google Gemini — suggesting these surfaces share underlying ranking signals.
