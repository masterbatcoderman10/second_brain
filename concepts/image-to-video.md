# Image-to-Video Generation

AI technique that transforms static images into video sequences, adding motion, emotion, and often sound to create living, breathing visual content.

## Overview

Image-to-video generation represents a multimodal capability where AI systems take a static image as input and generate a dynamic video sequence. This goes beyond simple animation — it involves understanding the image content and extrapolating plausible motion, temporal dynamics, and often audio.
