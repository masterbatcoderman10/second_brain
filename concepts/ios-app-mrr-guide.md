# iOS App Lazymaxxers MRR Guide

A tactical playbook for building a profitable iOS app to $10K MRR with minimal effort, by Will (@athcanft).

## Core Framework

**Thesis:** Don't innovate — replicate what's already working, add a viral feature, and let TikTok ads do the rest. No UGC. No influencers. No organic. Just ads, a paywall, and a nap.

The strategy hinges on three pillars: (1) studying proven app patterns rather than inventing from scratch, (2) triggering a core human desire with a screen-recordable "aha moment," and (3) aggressive pricing with a retention offer at cancellation.

## Methodology

### 1. Pattern Recognition Over Innovation
Examine top-grossing apps in target niches — Cal AI ($3M+/mo), UMax (face rating), Pray Screen (faith/prayer lock) — to identify what emotional trigger they're hitting, how they structure onboarding, and where the paywall sits in the flow. The goal is to extract the underlying pattern, not clone the app.

### 2. Core Desire Targeting
The deepest-converting desires: attractiveness, health, wealth, sex, status, intelligence, identity, fear. Apps triggering "Am I attractive?" convert far better than productivity apps. The viral feature must be screen-recordable — a reveal moment that sells itself in a single TikTok frame.

### 3. Pricing Architecture
- **Hard paywall:** No trial, no freemium — pay to access the desire
- **Primary:** $12.99/week + $59.99/year
- **Retention offer:** $44.99/year presented only at cancellation (Apple-approved method)
- **LTV benchmark:** ~$30+/paying user

### 4. Ad Production Workflow
Before/after AI-generated ads using fal.ai/z-image + SwapTok to TikTok Studio. One person produces 10-12 variants in an hour. Native/rough aesthetic, hook in 2 seconds, comment bait to drive TikTok engagement.

### 5. TikTok Smart+ Execution
- $50/day starting budget, USA only, optimize for purchases not installs
- 2-3 days to test creative viability
- Scale winners (+30% every 3 days max)
- Target metrics: CPI < $2.00, CTR > 1.00%, CPA < LTV

### 6. Leverage Programs
- TikTok ad credit: matches first $6,000 spend on new accounts
- Apple's Small Business Program: 15% fee instead of 30%
- Cashflow planning: Apple payouts have 45-day delay

## Key Insight
**One winning ad can carry a business for months.** The entire game is finding that one creative and scaling it. Most founders fail not because the product is bad but because they don't isolate the winning ad fast enough and iterate on it.
