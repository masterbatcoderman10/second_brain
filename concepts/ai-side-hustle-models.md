# AI Side Hustle Models

Six business models that generate $10K/month solo, none involving app development or coding:

## 1. Niche Newsletter Engine
- Pick specific industry (insurance brokers, dental practices, wedding planners)
- Write weekly newsletter on AI's impact on that industry
- 3 revenue streams: sponsorships, paid community, consulting
- Moat is niche specificity; general AI newsletters die

## 2. Digital Product Compound
- Build one small digital product every 2 weeks ($19-49 each)
- Templates, prompts, playbooks
- 18 products by month 9; not every product hits but 3-4 carry the catalog
- Each new product cross-promotes old ones

## 3. Workflow Licensing
- Build Make/n8n workflows for specific problems (lead research, content repurposing, support automation)
- License: $500 setup + $200/month per business
- 10 businesses × 1 workflow = $2K/month recurring
- 4 workflows × 40 businesses = $8-10K/month recurring

## 4. AI-Powered Research Reports
- Sell industry reports that used to take 3 months to produce
- AI does data pulls + drafting; human adds interpretation
- $500-2000 per report, 3-5/month

## 5. Executive Ghost-Writing
- Write content for CEOs/founders who want a voice but have no time
- AI drafts, human edits, executive approves
- $2500-5000/month retainer per executive, 3 clear $10K

## 6. Vertical Local Business Implementation
- Pick one vertical (chiropractors, real estate, law firms)
- Become the AI implementation person for that vertical
- $3000-5000 setup + $1000-2000/month retainer
- 5 setups + 5 retainers = $10K/month floor

## 7. AI-Powered Review Management (Boring Service)
- Handle all Google/Yelp/Facebook review responses for local businesses
- $1,500 setup + $700/month per location
- 10 locations = $7,000/month recurring
- AI drafts brand-voice responses; owner approves
- Immediate visible result; closes on first call
- See [[ai-review-management-service]] for full breakdown

## Common Thread
None sell AI as a product. All sell business outcomes. AI is invisible to buyer.
The skill stack that enables all: prompt engineering + context engineering + automation (Make/n8n).
