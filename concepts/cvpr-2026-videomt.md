# VidEoMT: Your ViT is Secretly Also a Video Segmentation Model

Video segmentation model demonstrating that a standard Vision Transformer (ViT) can perform video segmentation without architectural modifications.

## Overview

Presents VidEoMT, a video segmentation approach that repurposes a plain ViT for video segmentation tasks. The work shows that video segmentation capabilities are already present in standard ViT architectures and do not require specialized attention mechanisms.
