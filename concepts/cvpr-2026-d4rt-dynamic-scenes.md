# Efficiently Reconstructing Dynamic Scenes One D4RT at a Time

Dynamic scene reconstruction using differentiable renderers, processing one D4RT (Differentiable Digital TwinRasterizer?) at a time.

## Overview

Presents an approach for efficiently reconstructing dynamic scenes — scenes with moving objects — by processing one component at a time through a differentiable rendering pipeline. Focuses on computational efficiency for real-world dynamic scene capture.
