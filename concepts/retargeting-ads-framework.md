# Retargeting Ads Framework (George Clements)

A B2B agency retargeting system built around 3 Meta (Instagram + Facebook) custom audiences that capture warm prospects generated by organic content and cold traffic campaigns. Published by [[george-clem]] of Paid House in an X Article (May 2026).

## Core Thesis

Most agency owners run cold traffic campaigns and post organic content as two completely separate systems. The audiences both systems create — people who watched content, engaged with posts, clicked ads, or followed the page — sit completely unmonetised.

Retargeting ads connect the two systems. Agencies with this set up convert warm audiences at a fraction of the cost of cold traffic acquisition, with dramatically higher conversion rates.

## The Trust Gap

**Cold traffic** audiences have zero prior exposure. Every claim in the ad is evaluated with scepticism because there is no reference point for credibility. Cold traffic CACs are typically $1,000–$2,500 for B2B agency offers because multiple touchpoints are required before conversion.

**Warm audiences** — people who watched 60 seconds of organic content, engaged with a post, or clicked a cold traffic ad — have already made a micro-commitment. They have seen the brand's face, heard the positioning, and formed an impression. The trust gap is already partially closed.

Retargeting campaigns convert at meaningfully higher rates than cold traffic targeting the same offer. CPMs are often lower because the audience is smaller and more targeted.

## The 3 Custom Audiences

### Instagram Engagers (180-Day Window)

Captures everyone who engaged with the Instagram profile in the last 180 days — profile visits, post likes, comments, shares, saves, story interactions, and DMs. Broadest warm audience pool from organic content.

**Why 180 days:**
- 30-day window is too small — audiences too small to generate meaningful impression volume
- 365-day window includes people whose familiarity has faded
- 180 days balances recency with volume

This audience grows automatically as organic content accumulates engagements. No additional ad spend required on the acquisition side.

### Instagram Followers

Everyone who currently follows the Instagram account. A stronger signal of intent than a single engagement event — following means the person actively chose to see ongoing content.

Followers tend to be other industry people, potential clients evaluating over a longer time horizon, or people gradually moving toward a buying decision. Retargeting them with a direct offer catches them at the right warm moment.

### Facebook Page Engagers

Captures everyone who engaged with the Facebook page — watched, clicked, commented on, or interacted with cold traffic ads. This captures warm prospects that the Instagram audiences miss entirely.

Cold traffic audiences who clicked an ad but didn't book a call are arguably the warmest non-converting prospects in the funnel. Retargeting them 24–72 hours later closes a meaningful percentage without additional cold traffic spend.

## How the 3 Audiences Work Together

Each captures a distinct segment with minimal overlap, together covering the full surface area of the warm prospect pool:

| Audience | Source | Intent Level |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram Engagers | Organic content | Broad/warm |
| Instagram Followers | Follow action | High intent |
| Facebook Page Engagers | Cold traffic non-converters | Highest warmth |

Running retargeting against all 3 simultaneously means consistent follow-up messaging across every meaningful brand touchpoint.

## Creative and Offer Requirements

Retargeting creative operates under different constraints than cold traffic because the audience already knows who you are. Cold traffic creative must do significant trust-building before making an offer. Retargeting can be more direct, more offer-focused, and more specific about the next step.

**Best-performing formats for B2B agency retargeting:**
- Talking head ads addressing the audience directly, referencing content they've been watching
- Social proof ads leading with specific client results
- Direct offer ads presenting service, deliverable, and CTA with minimal warm-up

**The offer must be consistent with organic content positioning.** If content is about scaling agencies with paid ads, the retargeting offer should be the paid ads service, framed in the same language.

**Primary text handles warm-stage objections, not cold-stage objections:**
- Cold objections: "I don't know who this is" / "I don't understand what they do"
- Warm objections: "Is this right for my specific situation?" / "Have they done this for someone like me?" / "Is right now the right time?"

## Budget Allocation

**Starting:** $20–50/day depending on warm audience size. Monitor frequency closely in the first few weeks.

- Frequency below 2–3 per week per person → room to increase budget
- Frequency climbing above that → refresh creative or pull back

**Scaling:** As organic content builds engagement and cold traffic generates more Facebook interactions, warm audiences grow and the budget ceiling rises naturally. For agencies posting consistently and spending meaningfully on cold traffic, $100–200/day in retargeting is sustainable.

**Allocation principle:** Retargeting typically generates a much more favourable CAC than cold traffic. If cold traffic CAC is $2,000 and retargeting CAC is $500, retargeting deserves a higher share of budget than a small supplementary spend.

## Technical Setup (Meta Ads Manager)

1. **Create custom audiences** in Audiences → Create Audience → Custom Audience
   - Instagram Engagers: Instagram account → everyone who engaged → 180 days
   - Instagram Followers: Instagram account → people who follow → no window
   - Facebook Page Engagers: Facebook page → everyone who engaged → 180 days

2. **Build retargeting campaign:** conversions or leads objective. At ad set level, select custom audiences. Exclude existing client list.

3. **Creative:** 2–3 retargeting-specific creatives distinct from cold traffic. Shorter and more direct.

4. **Monitor frequency** weekly. Adjust based on audience absorption.

## Why It Compounds Over Time

Every piece of organic content adds new people to the Instagram engager audience. Every cold traffic ad that generates a click or comment adds new people to the Facebook page engager audience. Every new follower adds to the followers audience.

The retargeting system is fed new warm prospects without direct investment beyond the retargeting campaign cost. Its efficiency improves as the warm audience base grows.

Agencies that set this up early — while audiences are still small — build the infrastructure and creative iteration history that makes the system significantly more productive by the time audiences scale.
