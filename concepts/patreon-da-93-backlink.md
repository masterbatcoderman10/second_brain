# Patreon DA 93 Backlink Method

A zero-cost SEO tactic: get a high-authority backlink (DA 93) from Patreon by creating a free creator page and placing a link in the bio and a post.

## Method

1. Create a free Patreon creator page
2. Edit About section — add your URL in bio and website fields
3. Publish one post containing your link
4. The backlink from patreon.com (DA 93) passes SEO equity to your target site

## Claims

- Patreon DA: 93
- Time cost: ~2 minutes
- Financial cost: $0
