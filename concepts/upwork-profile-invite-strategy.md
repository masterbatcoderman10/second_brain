# Upwork Profile Invite Strategy

Ryan Ramshaw's method for getting 250+ client invites every 90 days on Upwork without sending outbound proposals. The strategy flips the typical outbound proposal grind into a passive inbound magnet by optimizing profile quality and niche positioning.

## Core Premise

Most freelancers on Upwork send mass proposals and get ignored. The alternative: become a profile that clients *want to reach out to*, making invites come to you. Ryan claims this approach drove his profile from $10K to $100K+ earned in under three months.

## The Three Levers

### 1. Niche Targeting

Before anything else: know exactly who you're targeting. Ryan recommends:

- Use Upwork's **search bar filtered by talent** to find competitors in your niche
- Study what clients they're working with and what pricing they command
- Use this intelligence to position yourself distinctly (not by copying, but by understanding the market)

### 2. Profile Completeness and Proof

Clients assess risk before messaging. Ryan emphasizes:

- **Loom video introduction** — builds trust faster than text; show personality and competence
- **Portfolio pieces** — concrete results, not just descriptions
- **Profile completeness** — every section filled signals professionalism
- The more proof, the lower the perceived risk for the client

### 3. Top Rated Badge

Ryan observed a correlation between:
- Getting the **Top Rated badge** → jump in invites
- **Maintaining Top Rated** → even bigger jump in invites

This badge signals verified performance to clients, reducing their hiring risk.

## The Mindset Principle

Ryan explicitly leads with mindset over mechanics: clients hire *people*, not templates. The frame shift is from "how do I send more proposals" to "how do I make clients want to work with me?"
