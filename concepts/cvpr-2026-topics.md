# CVPR 2026 Topics

Research topics covered in the [[skalski-p-top-cvpr-2026-papers]] curated paper list.

## Topics

### Segmentation

Techniques for segmenting images and videos at various granularities. Papers: [[cvpr-2026-videomt]], [[cvpr-2026-matanyone-2]], [[cvpr-2026-insid3]].

### 3D Vision

Reconstructing and understanding 3D scenes from 2D images or video. Papers: [[cvpr-2026-b3-seg]], [[cvpr-2026-d4rt-dynamic-scenes]].

### Object Tracking

Following objects across video frames over time. Paper: [[cvpr-2026-real-world-point-tracking]].

### Pose Estimation

Estimating human or object pose (3D keypoints) from images or video. Paper: [[cvpr-2026-fmpose3d]].

### Generative Models

Synthesizing video content, often from observation or conditioning signals. Paper: [[cvpr-2026-egox]].
