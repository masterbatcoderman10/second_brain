## Overview

Real-time World Models represent the next frontier in AI — systems capable of generating immersive worlds in real-time. Reactor World (@reactorworld) is building infrastructure to enable this capability with global low-latency distribution.

## Key Claims

- Real-time world generation is the next major AI frontier
- Early preview available for体验
- Global low-latency infrastructure powers the experience
- Worlds are generated in real-time as users interact

## Technical Implications

- Requires extremely low inference latency
- Needs globally distributed compute to minimize latency
- World model must maintain consistency across interactions
- Streaming/generative approach rather than pre-rendered
