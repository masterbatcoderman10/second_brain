# Cartoon UGC

A short-form content format that uses AI-generated cartoon characters (SpongeBob, Peppa Pig, Mr. Krabs, etc.) to promote e-commerce products on TikTok, Amazon, and Instagram. The cartoon characters serve as Pavlovian nostalgia triggers that stop the scroll; the product is woven into the narrative rather than presented directly.

## Why It Works
- **Nostalgia triggers** cause immediate attentional capture — viewers don't scroll past familiar characters they associate with positive memories
- **Familiarity lowers resistance**; laughter opens wallets
- Watch time → algorithm distribution → more eyeballs → more conversions
- Production cost: low; content volume potential: very high (one person can run multiple accounts)

## The Formula
1. **Hook first** — chaos at frame one, cartoon character in an unexpected situation
2. **Product woven in** — not a pitch, part of the story
3. **Feelings over features** — "I used to feel exhausted" not "this has 50mg of caffeine"
4. **One video, five placements:** organic TikTok, Spark Ad, Amazon Listing Video, Amazon Sponsored Brand Video, Instagram Reels

## Metrics
- A creator using this format hit **$500k views on their first video**, qualified for TikTok monetization in 6 days
- Rizz App paid $2M to faceless UGC creators → generated $15M+ revenue across 5B views

## Window
Early mover advantage is closing. Six-month horizon before saturation similar to early TikTok creator rewards and early Amazon video placements.
