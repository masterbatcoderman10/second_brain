# Claude Skills Service Business

Selling Claude Skills as a productized service business — build custom skills for clients and charge setup fees + monthly recurring revenue.

## Core Concept

Anthropic's Skill Creator lets non-technical users build reusable automations inside Claude from plain-English descriptions. The arbitrage: businesses need these but don't know they exist or how to build them. Sellers arbitrage that knowledge gap.

**Key distinction from prompts:** Prompts are one-time instructions; Skills are permanent workflows built once, executed forever on schedule or on-command.

## The 7 Skill Blueprints

| # | Skill | Setup Fee | Monthly | Core Function |
|---|-------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Brand Clone | $1,000-2,000 | $200/mo | Voice extraction from 20-30 content samples → Claude writes in exact brand voice |
| 2 | Weekly Autopilot Report | $1,500-3,000 | $300-500/mo | Friday automated reports from Google Sheets/Gmail/Notion via MCP |
| 3 | Content Atomizer | $2,000-3,500 | $500/mo | 1 long-form piece → 15+ platform-specific posts (tweets, LinkedIn, email, TikTok scripts) |
| 4 | Proposal Machine | $1,500-2,500 | $300/mo | 10-min intake form → complete proposal/SOW in client tone |
| 5 | Client Radar | $2,000-3,500 | $400/mo | CRM monitoring for renewals, churn risk, upsell signals with pre-drafted actions |
| 6 | Support Shield | $3,000-5,000 | $500/mo | Knowledge-base ingestion for AI-powered first-response support drafting |
| 7 | Outreach Engine | $2,500-4,000 | $400/mo | Personalized B2B prospecting at scale via company research |

## Revenue Math

- **One skill, 10 clients:** $73K year 1 ($25K upfront + $48K MRR)
- **Three skills, 30 clients:** $219K year 1

## Three Monetization Paths (No Custom Build Required)

1. **AI Employee Setup Service** ($3,000-5,000) — audit, recommend, configure, train. Margin ~100%.
2. **Niche-Specific Skill Packs** — bundle 3-5 skills per vertical (e.g., "Real Estate Agent Stack"). Higher price for narrower niche.
3. **Skill Workshops** ($300-500/person) — teach one niche to build their own. Recordings sell for $149; Q&A upsell at $49/mo.

## Decision Framework (Which to Build First)

| Situation | Start With | Why |
|-----------|------------|-----|
| Never sold a service | Proposal Machine (#4) | Use it for yourself first; it's both product and sales tool |
| Has existing clients | Weekly Autopilot Report (#2) | Fastest ROI, easiest upsell |
| Wants highest ticket | Support Shield (#6) | Boardroom conversation, budget exists |
| Wants fastest build | Brand Clone (#1) | 90 min, no connectors, foundation for all others |
