## End Goal

Produce AI-generated social media content (reels, stories, posts) for UberHaus that drives client inquiries via Instagram and Facebook. Prove the UGC model for interior design and use Waleed's work as a content engine.

## How Monetizable

- Client acquisition for UberHaus → direct revenue from interior design projects
- Content-as-distribution: once Waleed's process is documented, the content system can be sold as a service to other interior designers
- Secondary: AI-generated content packages sold to other home services businesses in Bangalore

## Vector

AI-generated ads (primary), AI-generated content (supporting)

## Target Market

- Primary: Bangalore educated working class (Instagram, Facebook users, 25–45 age range)
- Secondary: Other interior design businesses in Bangalore with zero content presence

## SRS: Product Requirements

### Content Types

- [ ] Before/after reveal reels (15–30s, vertical)
- [ ] Material spotlight posts: wood finishes, tiles, lighting — single image + caption
- [ ] Process timelapse: raw site → finished project (15–60s timelapse video)
- [ ] Client testimonial format: photo + voiceover or text overlay
- [ ] Design tips carousel posts (static image carousel, Instagram/Facebook)
- [ ] Reel hooks: 3–5 second hook variants per design project

### Production Workflow

- [ ] Input: project photos, material specs, dimensions from Waleed
- [ ] AI generation layer: Claude for script/hook generation, video generation tool for timelapse
- [ ] Voiceover: TTS or Waleed's voice note converted to content
- [ ] Caption generation: hook + body + CTA per post
- [ ] Hashtag set per post type (location-tagged, niche-tagged)
- [ ] Posting schedule: 3–5 posts/week across Instagram + Facebook

### Platform Requirements

- [ ] Instagram Reels + Feed posts
- [ ] Facebook Feed + Reels
- [ ] WhatsApp Status for warm audience amplification
- [ ] Link in bio → contact form or WhatsApp

### Tracking

- [ ] Reach and engagement per post
- [ ] Inquiries attributed to content (manual track initially: "found us on Instagram?")
- [ ] Cost per inquiry per platform
