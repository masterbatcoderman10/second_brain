# Niche-Specificity Digital Product Framework

A 4-step framework for turning domain expertise into sellable digital products by progressively narrowing audience specificity, sourced from [[dickie-bush]]'s Ship 30 for 30 playbook ($2M+ revenue).

## Core Principle

> "The riches are in the niches."

A product that tries to serve everyone serves no one. Specificity is the prerequisite for positioning, curriculum design, and marketing message resonance.

## The 3 Levels of Specificity

| Level | Example (Ship 30) | Why It Matters |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| Mega-Category | Writing | Too broad — includes all writers |
| Niche | Internet/Digital Writing | Narrows to people publishing online |
| **Niche-Within-Niche** | **Beginner Internet Writers** | Specific enough to name problems + outcomes |

Skipping specificity means building on generic, watered-down ideas that fail before launch.

## 4-Step Process

**Step 1:** Pick an industry → pick a niche → pick a niche-within-niche

**Step 2:** List the 10 biggest problems for that specific niche-within-niche
- Problems must be specific to the niche-within-niche, not generic
- e.g., for beginner writers: Distractions, Over-Editing, Perfectionism, Procrastination, Self-Confidence, Generating Ideas, Imposter Syndrome, Writing Consistently, Choosing A Platform, Finding Time To Write

**Step 3:** List the 10 most desirable outcomes for that same niche-within-niche
- Written as first-person "I" statements: *"If I wasn't constantly over-editing, I would save hundreds of hours and publish way more often"*
- Must be outcomes the creator has personally achieved or is working toward
- Transforms problems into curriculum and marketing copy

**Step 4:** Pick the most painful/expensive problem → create a digital product to solve it

## Why This Works

- **Positioning**: leads with ONE problem, not 10. The 10 problems inform curriculum and secondary messaging.
- **Curriculum**: specificity naturally produces action-oriented content vs. generic theory
- **Marketing**: "I" statement outcomes become ad copy and landing page language
- **Domain expertise**: creator must have personally experienced the niche-within-niche to teach it credibly
