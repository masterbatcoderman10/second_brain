# AI Business Models 2026

Filtered list of viable AI business models at different revenue tiers, with dead models explicitly called out.

## Core Thesis

Most AI courses in 2026 recycle dead business models: dropshipping with AI, print-on-demand with AI, content farms. The models that work require real execution, not prompt templates.

## Revenue Tiers

### $1M+/Month Tier (Hard to start, impossible to copy)
- **Micro SaaS portfolio**: 10 small AI apps, one hit pays $50K-$250K/month
- **Service as Software**: sell finished results, not tool access ($200/mo replaces $60K/year employee)
- **Buy a boring business**: laundromat/car wash/resort, automate 30-50% of ops, business worth 3x purchase price
- **AI cybersecurity**: 24/7 phishing/deepfake protection, corporate contracts, almost no competition
- **Venture AI studio / AI agent marketplace**: factory for launching startups or "Airbnb for AI agents"

### $10K-$50K/Month Tier (Real money, real clients)
- **AI automation agency**: wire up AI systems for email/leads/reports, visible ROI in first conversation
- **Lead generation**: AI agents parsing databases + personalized cold outreach at scale
- **Voice/chat agents**: 24/7 answering and booking for service businesses, monthly subscription
- **Corporate AI training**: $5K-$10K for two days, teaching teams Claude in real workflows
- **AI ad agency**: video campaigns via Midjourney/Cling/Sora, $1K-$100K per campaign

### Starter Tier (Under $10K/month, where you learn)
- **Digital products**: prompt libraries, Notion templates, mini guides on Gumroad
- **Faceless content**: real storytelling + dynamic editing + TikTok Shop integration
- **Micro audience acquisition**: buy small email lists, monetize with AI products
- **Freelance AI services**: SEO blogs, thumbnails, Shorts cuts, voiceovers

## Avoid Completely
- AI trading bots (if they worked, wouldn't be sold in $97 course)
- Cheap AI copywriting, data entry, generic subtitles
- Print-on-demand (saturated with AI-generated prints)

## Launch Formula
1. Find the problem first
2. Pre-sell before building
3. Deliver manually using AI tools
4. Only then build the system
5. Automation comes after consistent paying clients, not before

## Bonus: Underrated Model
Mobile apps for offline cafes/restaurants:
- Push notifications for new menu items
- Built-in delivery orders
- Loyalty mechanics (10% off for regulars)
- $300-500 setup + $100-200/month ongoing
- One city, 20 cafes = $2K-$4K/month recurring
