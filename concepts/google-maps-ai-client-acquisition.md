# Google Maps AI Client Acquisition

Three-part AI-powered service business model using ChatGPT 5.5 + Google Maps to find, qualify, and close local service business clients at scale.

## Overview

Local service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pest control) have high profit margins but poor digital presence. This workflow uses AI to identify targets, build websites, manage reputation, and automate outreach — generating $5,000–15,000/month with zero prior experience.

## Part 1 — Website Business ($500–2,000/client)

1. Search Google Maps for "HVAC near me" / "plumber near me"
2. Filter for businesses with good ratings but no/outdated website
3. Extract business name, services, and top reviews
4. Prompt ChatGPT 5.5 to generate a complete website brief
5. Build in AI website builder (Framer, Webflow, Wix AI, Lovable) in under an hour
6. Show live preview → close on the spot

Pricing:
- Basic website (5 pages): $500–800
- Full website + booking: $1,000–1,500
- Website + monthly maintenance: $1,200 upfront + $200/month

## Part 2 — Reputation Management ($300–800/month/client)

Target: businesses with under 4.2 stars on Google Maps.

Service:
- Monitor Google Maps reviews
- Generate human-like responses via ChatGPT 5.5
- Implement positive review generation system
- Business owner does nothing

ChatGPT prompt for review responses:
```
You are a professional reputation manager for [Business Name],
a [type of business] in [city].

Write a professional, warm and personal response to this Google review:
[paste the review]

Guidelines:
- Thank them by first name if mentioned
- Address any specific complaint directly and professionally
- Offer to make it right if negative
- Under 150 words, sound human not corporate
- End with an invitation to return or contact directly
```

## Part 3 — Cold Outreach on Autopilot (500 leads/day)

Three-step automated pipeline:

1. **Scrape**: Outscraper or PhantomBuster pull business names, emails, phone, ratings, review counts from any Google Maps search → spreadsheet of 200 businesses in 10 minutes
2. **Qualify**: ChatGPT 5.5 filters for ratings 3.5–4.2, 20+ reviews, no/outdated website, in target categories
3. **Outreach**: ChatGPT 5.5 writes personalized cold emails referencing specific business data (rating, review count, missing website)

Conversion math:
- 500 emails/day × 3% response = 15 interested businesses/day
- 10% close rate = 1–2 new clients/day
