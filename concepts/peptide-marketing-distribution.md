# Peptide Marketing Distribution Strategy

## Summary

Guide by [[roman_khaves]] on winning peptide marketing distribution via three content formats: human UGC, AI slideshows, and AI UGC videos. Peptide customers spend $2K-$4K/year with 7-14 year retention, enabling 5x creator payouts.

## Key Points

- **Market opportunity**: Ozempic/Mounjaro generated $71B in 2025 (vs OpenAI + Anthropic combined at $29B)
- **Distribution problem**: Ecom founders fighting over same meta/google keywords at $200 CPMs, rejected for "compounded medication"
- **Customer LTV**: $2,000-$4,000/year, 7-14 year protocol retention once established
- **Creator payment**: 5x standard vertical rates still profitable due to LTV math

## Three Content Formats

### 1. Human UGC at Scale
- Real human face converts best for health decisions (parasocial trust)
- Simplest: creator in kitchen discussing problem (sleep, joint pain, inflammation, fat loss)
- Cheaper version: zero talking, simple movements + overlay text
- Deliberate misspellings (r3ta, p3ptid3s) dodge keyword detection
- CPM: $1.50-$3 on AffiliateNetwork.com; zero risk, fully performance-based

### 2. AI Slideshows
- 70%+ completion rates vs 40% for video on TikTok
- Formats: before/after, metric progression, problem/solution carousels, list breakdowns, contrarian hooks, ironic reverse lists, assumed adoption, lifestyle proof
- Production: Nano Banana Pro for images (iPhone color grade), Claude for hooks (reddit-sourced, negative constraints), Canva/CapCut for assembly
- Cost: under $1/slideshow once dialed in

### 3. AI UGC Videos
- Mascot explainer: 3D character with face, name, signature prop; vertical 9:16, Pixar-style 3D, <30 sec, max 3 scene swaps
- Realistic AI UGC: Seedance 2.0 or Veo 3.1 rendering, Claude scripts with negative constraints
- Sourced through AffiliateNetwork.com (150,000+ creators)
