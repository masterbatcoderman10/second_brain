# AI Cartoon UGC Monetization

## Definition

Using AI-generated cartoon characters (SpongeBob, Peppa Pig, etc.) as the format for UGC-style content on TikTok, Instagram, and Amazon to drive product sales. The cartoon acts as a Pavlovian trigger — familiarity stops the scroll — while the product is woven into the story.

## Core Mechanism

1. **Stop the scroll**: Familiar cartoon characters create instant recognition and nostalgia; viewers stop rather than scroll past
2. **Lower resistance**: Familiarity + entertainment = emotional state where selling becomes effortless
3. **Scale via AI**: Script → published video in under 60 minutes; consistent characters, consistent brand voice, infinitely scalable vs. manual creator ceilings

## Format Structure

- **Hook first, product second** — entertainment hook with familiar character, product enters naturally inside the scene
- **Script for feelings, not features** — three emotional notes: make them laugh, make them feel seen, create sudden recognition
- **One video, five placements**:
  1. Organic TikTok → TikTok Shop traffic
  2. TikTok Spark Ad → amplify organic
  3. Amazon Listing Video → product page
  4. Amazon Sponsored Brand Video → search results
  5. Instagram Reels → Meta retargeting
