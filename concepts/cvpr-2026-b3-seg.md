# B³-Seg: Camera-Free, Training-Free 3DGS Segmentation

Segmentation of 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) scenes without cameras or training, using analytic EIG and Beta-Bernoulli Bayesian updates.

## Overview

B³-Seg segments objects within 3D Gaussian Splatting reconstructions without requiring camera parameters or additional training. Uses analytic Expected Information Gain (EIG) to select informative viewpoints and Beta-Bernoulli Bayesian updates for robust segmentation refinement. Marked as a highlighted paper.
