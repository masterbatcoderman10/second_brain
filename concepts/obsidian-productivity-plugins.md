# Obsidian Productivity Plugins

The Obsidian plugin ecosystem for productivity, time management, and knowledge work. Community plugins extend Obsidian from plain note-taking into a full personal productivity system.

## Common Pain Points

**Vault fragmentation** — newer users (under 1 month) hit organizational friction as their vault grows. Plugins multiply and notes scatter across folders.

**Enterprise calendar sync** — syncing Outlook/Google calendar into Obsidian remains difficult in 2026:
- ICS publishing from Outlook Web is often blocked by enterprise IT
- CalDAV returns 503 errors on institutional Microsoft 365 accounts

**The productivity loop** — community concern that time spent configuring plugins exceeds time using them for actual work.
