# AI Review Management Service

Productized AI-powered customer review response service for local businesses. A "boring" but high-demand service positioned as a $7K/month recurring revenue opportunity for solo operators.

## The Service

- Handle every Google, Yelp, Facebook, and industry-site review for a local business
- AI drafts responses matching brand voice; owner approves and ships
- 24-hour turnaround promise
- Immediate visible result: every review gets a response

## Pricing Model

| Component | Price |
|-----------|-------|
| One-time setup | $1,500 |
| Monthly retainer per location | $700 |
| **10 locations** | **$7,000/month** |

Alternative: 3 multi-location groups (3-4 locations each) = same revenue, fewer relationships.

## Time Commitment

5-8 hours per week per client at scale. Most time is review approvals + monthly report.

## Setup Work (Week 1)

1. Audit every existing review
2. Build tone and brand-voice file
3. Set up AI response pipeline
4. Write 20-50 response templates: positive, neutral, negative, escalation
5. System responds to every review within 4 hours

## Pitch Process

1. List 30 local businesses with poor review response rates (restaurants, dental, auto, real estate)
2. Send 4-line cold pitch: "Saw your last 30 Google reviews have no response. We handle this for [vertical] at $700/month. Setup is $1,500. Reviews start getting answered within 24 hours. Worth a call?"
3. Run calls; close 1-2 out of 30 in first week

## Why Now

- Local businesses know AI exists but don't know how to use it
- Agencies pitch $5K websites or $3K retainers they can't afford
- $700/month productized service on a known pain point is exactly what they're ready for
- Supply is tiny; price floor will compress by Q4 2026

## Required Skill Stack

- Prompt engineering (brand-voice response drafting)
- Context engineering (tone files)
- Make / n8n (review feed → response pipeline wiring)
