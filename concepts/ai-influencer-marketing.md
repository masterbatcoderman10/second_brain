# AI Influencer Marketing

Using AI-generated virtual creators (avatars) to promote apps and software products on social media, primarily Instagram and TikTok.

## Core Mechanics

**Why it works:** AI influencers are cheap ($39 for an avatar), fast to produce, don't demand payment or equity, and can be scaled to 5+ accounts simultaneously. The content format — usually short-form video — drives organic reach when volume is applied.

**The 3-box framework** ([[ernesto-lopez]]):
1. Pick a painkiller app (solves a specific, felt problem)
2. Build killer onboarding (converts the traffic once it arrives)
3. Add a good CTA (explicit call to action in every video)

**Algorithm strategy:** Post 10+ reels per day. Most will underperform; 1 in 10 or 1 in 100 goes viral and drives outsized downloads. Volume + speed + research on what's working = the formula.

**CTAs that work:** Direct download links, "start free trial" moments woven into the video narrative.
