# ChatGPT SEO Ranking

A 100-day methodology for ranking content on AI search engines like ChatGPT. Developed by [[denohawari]] and documented in his X Article (May 2026).

## Core Principles

- **Quality over volume** — AI ranks brands with clearest explanations, not most content
- **Answer-first structure** — Direct answers in first 50 words, then how/why, then supporting content
- **Topic authority** — Systematic coverage of every question variation within a niche
- **Entity signals** — AI looks for cross-site mentions, references, and consistent messaging to establish trust

## Three Phases

### Phase 1: Solution Validation (Days 1–30)
Extract pain points from sales calls, Reddit threads, Twitter mentions, and support tickets. Write 10–15 "answer-first" pieces targeting "What is X?", "How to do X?", "Why does X fail?" Distribute on social and track time on page, session length, and site clicks.

### Phase 2: Programmatic Coverage (Days 31–70)
Break core topics into angles (definitions, comparisons, use cases, mistakes, advanced tactics). Build content clusters with pillar pages and supporting pieces. Maintain consistent terminology so AI associates the brand with the topic.

### Phase 3: Entity Velocity (Days 71–100)
Get mentioned on external sites (guest posts, podcasts, interviews). Create "referenceable assets" — original frameworks, named concepts, data points, contrarian takes. Keep messaging tightly focused on one core topic.

## Metrics

- AI search conversion: 23× higher than organic (SERPs.io study)
- LLM share of search: 12–15% currently → estimated 25–28% by end of 2026
