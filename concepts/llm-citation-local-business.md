# LLM Citation for Local Businesses

The emerging practice of positioning a local service business to be recommended by AI citation models (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) rather than ranking in traditional search.

## What It Is

When potential customers ask "who should I call" to an AI assistant instead of searching Google, the AI cites sources it trusts — comparison content, Reddit threads, honest blog posts. Traditional Google Ads and lead-reselling platforms (HomeAdvisor, Angi, Thumbtack) are invisible to these models.

## Key Dynamics

- **47%** of local customers now use AI assistants instead of search (Pew Research, March 2026)
- Google AI Overview appears on **87%** of local search queries
- Lead-reselling platforms declining: HomeAdvisor -71%, Angi restructuring, Thumbtack -23% staff
- AI models cite **comparison content**, not ads

## The Fix

1. Write honest comparison content ("best X in city" listicles, "real pricing for Y" pages)
2. Post helpful Reddit/forum comments that demonstrate expertise
3. Track citations at ranking tools
4. Compounding effect: every week of content builds the citation lead

## Why It's Different from Traditional SEO

Traditional SEO = backlinks, meta descriptions, keyword density → invisible to AI citation models

LLM citation = honest, specific, comparative content that AI models can verify and cite → directly drives recommendations
