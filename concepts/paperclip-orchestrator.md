# Paperclip Orchestrator (Marketing Event — Different Project)

## Overview

An AI orchestrator designed to manage teams of AI agents (like Hermes). It functions as a "CEO" to the agents' "employees".

## Key Capabilities
- **Task Assignment:** Distributes work to plugged-in models.
- **Strategic Planning:** Develops org charts and plans based on company mission.
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** Sends text messages for user approval on budgets and plans (Board of Directors model).
- **Multi-Client Support:** Can run multiple "clients" (e.g., different brands) with specialized skill sets (SEO, Ads, Social).
- **API Integration:** Exposes an API, allowing other agents (like Hermes) to programmatically interact or even replace the human approver.
