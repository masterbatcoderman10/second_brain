# design-md-for-agents

[[design-md-for-agents]] is the concept of training AI agents on well-structured DESIGN.md files to improve their design generation capabilities. By exposing agents to curated examples from products with excellent design systems, they learn patterns for colors, typography, spacing, and layouts.

Projects like [[refero-styles]] aggregate DESIGN.md files from top products into datasets specifically formatted for model training. This approach recognizes that agents produce better outputs when they have access to quality reference material.

This practice is related to [[google-labs-code-design]] initiatives and tools like [[huashu-design]] and [[pencil]] that provide design guidance for AI systems.
