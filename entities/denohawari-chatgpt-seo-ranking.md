# How To Rank On ChatGPT in 100 Days (Complete Roadmap)

X Article by [[denohawari]] documenting a 100-day roadmap for ranking on ChatGPT and AI search engines. The article outlines three phases: Solution Validation (Days 1–30), Programmatic Coverage (Days 31–70), and Entity Velocity (Days 71–100).

## Key Claims

- AI search converts 23× higher than organic search
- LLM traffic estimated to grow from 12–15% to 25–28% of all searches by end of 2026
- Musicfy case study: 692K clicks and $1M+ revenue in a few months
- Language learning brand: 1,700% increase in organic visibility, $400K+ revenue from LLM SEO

## Three-Phase Framework

1. **Days 1–30: Solution Validation** — Extract pain points from sales calls, Reddit, Twitter, support tickets; write 10–15 "answer-first" pieces; distribute on social
2. **Days 31–70: Programmatic Coverage** — Expand winning angles into full topic clusters; systematic coverage of every question variation
3. **Days 71–100: Entity Velocity** — Get mentioned across multiple external sites; create referenceable assets (original frameworks, named concepts, data); maintain tight messaging consistency
