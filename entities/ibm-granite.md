# IBM Granite

IBM's family of open-source AI models under the Granite brand. Covers language models (Granite 4.0 series), speech models (Granite Speech), and vision-language models. Apache 2.0 license across the family.

## Granite Model Family

### Language Models
- **[[granite-4-0-1b-base]]** — 1B parameter dense base model used as the LLM backbone for Granite Speech models

### Speech Models
- **[[granite-speech-4-1-2b]]** — base variant: punctuation, capitalization, AST, keyword-biased recognition, Japanese support
- **[[granite-speech-4-1-2b-plus]]** — plus variant: speaker-attributed ASR + word-level timestamps (no punctuation/capitalization)
- **[[granite-speech-4-1-2b-nar]]** — non-autoregressive variant for higher throughput
