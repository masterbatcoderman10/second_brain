# Alventra Marketing

## Overview

Alventra Marketing is a local SEO-focused marketing agency founded by [[bloggersarvesh]]. The agency specializes in AI-augmented SEO workflows for home services businesses (plumbers, HVAC, contractors, lawyers, cleaning companies).

Core offering: running a 20-prompt [[claude-cowork-seo-system]] for local businesses — auditing their entire Google presence, finding competitor gaps, and executing a prioritized SEO plan over 90 days.

## Key Claims

- Helped home services businesses generate hundreds of thousands in new revenue using this framework
- System replaces half-day manual audits per client, completing them in minutes
- 90-day consistent execution can outrank businesses established for years

## Business Model

Agency model: selling SEO execution as a retainer service. Alventra runs the full 20-prompt audit stack for clients, including competitor analysis, GBP optimization, website fixes, backlink building, and monthly reporting.
