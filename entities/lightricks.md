# Lightricks

## Overview

Lightricks is an Israeli AI company known for its creative AI tools (Photomosh, VScope, etc.) and open-source contributions. It developed **LTX-2** and **[[ltx-2-3]]**, a family of DiT-based audio-video foundation models.

## Key Facts

- **Headquarters:** Israel
- **Open-source models:** LTX-2, LTX-2.3 (with associated LoRAs and upscalers)
- **Paper:** LTX-2: Efficient Joint Audio-Visual Foundation Model (arXiv:2601.03233, 2025)
- **Repository:** github.com/Lightricks/LTX-2
- **Demo:** app.ltx.studio
- **API:** console.ltx.video

## Products

- **[[ltx-2-3]]** — DiT-based audio-video generation model; 22B parameters; joint audio-video generation; ComfyUI nodes available; Diffusers support incoming
- LTX-Video (prior version, referenced in [[open-higgsfield-ai]])
