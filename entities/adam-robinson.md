# Adam Robinson

X creator (@retentionadam) and founder with a track record of building 3 software companies to over **$35M in combined ARR**.

Author of the viral X Article *"How to Launch an AI Company From 0 Today (And Scale to $1M+)"* (January 2026), which distilled his hard-won lessons on customer discovery, staying manual before automating, and identifying PMF signals.

## Core Methodology

Adam's startup methodology centers on **discovery before building**, emphasizing pre-payment validation over feature development. Key elements:

- **50 discovery calls minimum** before writing any code
- **Pre-payment validation**: swipe actual customer cards before building a website
- **Manual-first**: stay manual as long as possible to stay close to customers and iterate faster
- **3 PMF signals**: weekly referrals, customer distress if product removed, organic growth without marketing
- **Build in public**: LinkedIn from day one to create awareness before outreach

## Current Company

RB2B (referral-to-revenue platform). Adam has referenced RB2B in multiple posts, noting that they manually sent spreadsheets over email for 5 months before launching a website — as deliberate [[manual-first-validation]] practice.
