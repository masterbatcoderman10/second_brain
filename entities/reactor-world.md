# Reactor World

AI company working on real-time world models. Early preview available. Being built by [[_bschmidtchen]] and team.

## Overview

Real-time world models are positioned as the next frontier in AI generation — models that can generate and render structured 3D world representations in real-time, as opposed to batch generation of video frames. Reactor World is the first company to announce an early preview targeting this space.
