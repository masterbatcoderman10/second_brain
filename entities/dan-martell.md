# Dan Martell

Dan Martell (@danmartell) is a serial entrepreneur and AI venture studio founder who went from a broke 24-year-old to building a $100M/year AI venture studio over 29 years.

## Profile

- **Handle:** @danmartell
- **Claimed achievement:** $100M/year AI venture studio
- **Core teaching:** Strategic relationship building as a primary monetization and growth lever

## Key Relationships Mentioned

- **Taki Moore** — Personal brand and content strategy collaborator; mutual coaching relationship over 8+ years
- **John Maxwell** — Leadership author/speaker; Dan flew 3,000 miles for a 28-minute conversation; Maxwell's "God-sized goals" concept was a pivotal influence
- **Rob Dyrdek** — Skateboarder/entrepreneur; reached out to Dan after reading his books; Dan had written Dyrdek's name on his intentional relationship goal list before they connected

## Core Methodology

Dan's central thesis is that relationship-building is a learnable skill that outperforms sales, marketing, and AI in terms of income generation. His four-part framework covers:

1. **Steel Sharpens Steel** — Reciprocal high-level relationships that multiply skills and expand thinking
2. **It Pays to Be Intentional** — Writing down relationship goals and revisiting monthly
3. **Relationships Unlock God-Sized Goals** — Setting goals so big they force relationship-building
4. **Content as Relationship Engine** — Publishing as a signal flare to attract the right people
