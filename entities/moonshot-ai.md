# Moonshot AI

Chinese AI company (also known as Moonshot AI / 月之暗面) behind the Kimi family of models including [[kimi-k2.6]]. Known for long-context language models and the Kimi platform (kimi.com).

## Products

- **Kimi K2.6** — Latest model featuring 300-sub-agent swarm architecture
- **Kimi K2.5** — Previous generation; ~100 sub-agents, ~1,300 coordinated steps
- **Kimi platform** — Consumer AI assistant at kimi.com with free and Allegretto paid tiers

## Known For

- 256k context window (among the longest in production)
- Open-source model releases under Modified MIT License
- Cloudflare Workers AI integration for free API access
- Agent swarm architecture for parallelized task execution
