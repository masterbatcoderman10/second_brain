## Source

## Key Thesis

Build AI agency services by deeply understanding one vertical's painful workflow — not by becoming a generalist "AI agent" freelancer.

Core argument:
- The viral AI agency playbook (pick one vertical, learn the workflows, become the team) is correct but too vague
- The actual money requires pointing to one painful workflow inside one business type and owning it completely
- AI agents succeed not by being capable but by being reversible — trust features matter more than capability features
