# Dimitar Angelov

X creator (@dimitarangg) focused on B2B lead generation using AI tools.

## Overview

Makes the case that Apollo.io leads are oversaturated (250M contacts hammered by thousands of agencies), and that the alternative is scraping untapped databases — LinkedIn job-changers, Twitter discussions of pain points, Reddit problem-seekers, Crunchbase-funded startups, Product Hunt makers, and more.

Argues that Claude Opus 4.7's visual accuracy upgrade enables scraping platforms that were previously too difficult.

## Key Claims

- Apollo leads have 2% reply rates because they've been pitched 50+ times
- Solution: scrape platforms where ICP actually congregates but competitors haven't reached
- 15+ untapped database categories listed (LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit, Crunchbase, Product Hunt, Discord/Slack, Google Maps, universities, events, etc.)
- Offer: scraped 10,000+ untapped B2B leads via done-for-you service
