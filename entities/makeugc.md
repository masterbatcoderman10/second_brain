# MakeUGC

## Overview

[[MakeUGC]] is an AI UGC ad-production platform used as the operational layer in the [[ai-ugc-ad-scaling-system]] described by [[stijn-feijen]]. In the source thread, it handles scheduling, publishing, creative organization, testing, and scaling. The linked homepage positions it as a platform to create AI UGC videos with [[seedance-2-0]] integrated into the workflow.

## Role in the source workflow

According to the ingested thread, [[makeugc]] is the system's execution engine:

- It hosts [[seedance-2-0]] video generation
- It automates publishing across TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Meta ads
- It manages multiple creatives simultaneously
- It provides the environment for daily test cycles, performance review, and scale-up

The source explicitly argues that the workflow "breaks" without this operational layer.

## Homepage claims captured during ingest

Claims visible on makeugc.ai during ingest:

- "#1 Platform to Create AI UGC"

- 300+ brands with $100M+ in revenue
