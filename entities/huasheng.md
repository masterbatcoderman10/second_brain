## About

Huasheng is the author of [[huashu-design]]. His philosophy for huashu-design: *"Type. Hit enter. A finished design lands in your lap."* He created the skill after playing with Claude Design at launch and realizing he preferred having an agent work in his terminal rather than opening a graphical UI.

He describes the skill as making *"the graphics-tool layer disappear"* — positioning it as complementary to Claude Design rather than competitive: Claude Design is the better graphics tool, but huashu-design removes the need to use one at all for terminal-first users.
