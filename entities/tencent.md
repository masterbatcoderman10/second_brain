# Tencent

Chinese tech conglomerate (SEHK: 0700). Robotics X + HY Vision Team develop foundation models for embodied AI and translation.

## Overview

Tencent is a Chinese multinational conglomerate known for social media (WeChat), gaming, cloud services, and AI research. The HY Vision Team focuses on vision foundation models while Robotics X works on physical AI systems.
