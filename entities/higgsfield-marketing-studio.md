# Higgsfield Marketing Studio

Marketing platform with Hermes Agent + Seedance 2.0 inside. One paste of product URL → 500+ ad-ready cuts per day.

Six steps:
1. Paste product URL/website/App Store page
2. Pick AI persona (upload your face or generate one)
3. Hermes Agent researches hooks converting in category
4. Seedance 2.0 renders videos with captions, pacing, hooks
5. Output: 500+ cuts in 9:16, 1:1, 16:9
6. Hermes tracks which hooks land, compounds winners
