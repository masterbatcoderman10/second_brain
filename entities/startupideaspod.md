# The Startup Ideas Podcast

AI-focused podcast ( @startupideaspod ) that publishes interviews with AI builders and creators, exploring practical applications of AI tools in product development and marketing.

## Source

[Seedance 2.0: The New Default Video Model](https://x.com/startupideaspod/status/2045221480120885529) — Interview with Sirio (an AI creative builder) walking through Seedance 2.0's multi-input video generation workflow, prompting techniques, and production use cases.

## About

The SIP episode covered Seedance 2.0 as a director's tool rather than a simple clip generator, featuring live demonstrations of virtual try-on, AI influencer product reviews, and green screen replacement workflows. Sirio's key thesis: the shift from generation to direction changes the input shape from "text or image, pick one" to "what have you got and how should I combine it."
