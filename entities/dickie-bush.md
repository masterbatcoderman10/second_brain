# Dickie Bush

X/Twitter creator (@dickiebush) known for writing, digital product monetization, and Ship 30 for 30.

## Background

Former Wall Street analyst who began posting on X in mid-2020, writing daily threads curating takeaways from podcasts. After 27 days of near-zero traction, his 28th thread went viral after being retweeted by Naval Ravikant.

## Digital Products

### Podcast Compendium ($400)

First digital product — a Notion document with podcast summaries sold on Gumroad for $29. Taught three lessons: no large audience needed to launch, first product should be work already done, people pay for organized thinking.

### Ship 30 for 30 ($2,000,000+)

Daily writing accountability cohort co-founded with Nicolas Cole. First cohort launched with 50+ paying members. Grew to 7 figures in revenue within 12 months. The product targets "beginner writers starting to write online" — a specific niche-within-niche (Mega-Category: Writing → Niche: Internet/Digital Writing → Niche-Within-Niche: Beginners).

## Claims

- Over $20,000,000 generated from digital products over 6 years (source claim, unverified)
- $2,000,000+ from Ship 30 for 30 alone (source claim)
- First cohort of Ship 30 hit 7 figures within 12 months (source claim)
