X/Twitter creator (handle: @spwfeijen) posting operator-focused workflows for AI-generated short-form advertising. - use Claude to generate hooks, angles, and 15-second UGC-style scripts
- generate video assets with [[seedance-2-0]]
- schedule, publish, test, and scale creatives inside [[makeugc]]
- review CTR, watch time, and conversion data daily
