# Boring Local SEO

X creator (@boringlocalseo) focused on local service business marketing in the AI era.

## Overview

Makes the case that local service businesses are being left behind as AI citation models (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) replace traditional search — and that the fix is simple: honest comparison content that AI models can cite, replacing expensive lead-buying and Google Ads.

## Key Claims

- 47% of local service customers now ask AI "who should I call" instead of Google search (Pew Research, March 2026)
- AI models cite comparison content, Reddit threads, and honest blog posts — NOT Google Ads
- Lead-reselling platforms (HomeAdvisor -71%, Angi restructuring, Thumbtack -23%) are collapsing
- Google AI Overview appears on 87% of local search queries
- The fix: one honest listicle, one pricing transparency page, 3-5 helpful Reddit comments
