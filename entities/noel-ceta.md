# Noel Ceta

## Overview

X/Twitter creator focused on SEO execution, especially building topical authority for new sites through narrow niche selection, topic mapping, pillar-cluster architecture, internal linking, and expertise signaling. In the current wiki, his main documented contribution is the [[topical-authority-seo]] framework, which overlaps with the site-structure logic behind [[programmatic-seo]] and can act as a foundation for later [[llm-seo]] work.

## Documented thread

### Topical authority from scratch (2026-04-13)

In a 14-post thread, Noel Ceta lays out a seven-step process for getting a new site to compete with higher-authority domains:

1. Choose a narrow niche.
2. Map the topic landscape.
3. Build pillar-cluster architecture.
4. Publish comprehensive content.
5. Establish internal linking.
6. Demonstrate expertise signals.
7. Earn niche-relevant backlinks.

The thread's strongest operational details are the topic-map requirement (50-100 subtopics), the pillar/cluster linking structure, and the insistence that every new piece should link to 3-5 existing pieces.

## Evidence quality
