# Building Effective Agents

Research paper by [[eric-mishra]] and Barry Zhang at [[Anthropic]]. Canonical guide to building agents across any application domain. Widely cited as foundational reference for agentic AI development.

## Relationship to AI Agent

This paper and [[eric-mishra]]'s work provide the conceptual foundation for what the [[agent]] concept page describes at a high level. The paper's four recommended practices (model your domain, give agents tools, design agentic loops, and ensure the human stays in the loop) are the concrete instantiation of the generic AI agent properties listed in [[agent]].
