# maxwellcopy-ecommerce-funnel

Maxwellcopy-ecommerce-funnel is a long-form X Article by Max Sturtevant (@maxwellcopy) published May 5, 2026, documenting the ecommerce funnel playbook used by billion-dollar DTC subscription brands including AG1, Grüns, and IM8 Health. The article breaks down six funnel stages: acquisition offers (value-stacked subscription-only deals), ad volume and creative mix (1,000–1,300+ live ads with heavy AI-generated static and influencer collab video), custom pain-point landing pages (10–20 pages per brand such as gut-health, science, feel-again, why-im8, competitive), quiz-based email capture pop-ups that surface the existing offer without extra discounting, aggressive email/SMS flows that convert before competitors retarget, and post-purchase education-first retention that lets the product do the work. The article went viral with 280 likes and 18 retweets.
