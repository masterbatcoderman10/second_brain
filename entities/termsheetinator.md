### GTM: The Unreasonable Effectiveness of HTML Lead Magnets (2026-05-10)
An article arguing that single-file HTML pages outperform PDFs as GTM assets for outbound and demand generation.

**Core thesis:** HTML lead magnets (self-contained, interactive, browser-opened) signal effort and customisation in a way PDFs cannot — and can be built in minutes with Claude Code.

**Six GTM use cases:**
1. Lead magnets that demonstrate instead of claiming
2. Custom proposals prospects actually read
3. Pre-demonstration deliverables that change deal velocity
4. Interactive prospect-specific wikis
5. Competitive analysis documents
6. Nurturing sequences

**Key claim:** A PDF signals "we put in effort before we knew you existed." HTML signals custom work done specifically for the prospect.
