# Qwen2VL 2B PNG-to-SVG GGUF

Experimental GGUF-quantized Qwen2-VL 2B model for image-to-SVG conversion on Apple Silicon. Referenced as the first GGUF vision model capable of serving a vision-to-vector pipeline locally.

## Status
