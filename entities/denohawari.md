# denohawari

## Overview

X/Twitter creator focused on programmatic SEO, organic growth, and content marketing systems. Runs [[trailblazer-marketing]] (trailblazermktg.com), a marketing agency offering free discovery calls. Shares detailed playbooks for building search traffic at scale using [[programmatic-seo]] patterns and newer [[llm-seo]] positioning for AI-mediated discovery.

## Key Content

### Programmatic SEO Thread (2026-04-13)

The canonical thread on programmatic SEO — 10 patterns for building pages at scale:

1. **Best pages (Curation)** — best [category], top [tools]
2. **Comparisons** — [x] vs [y], [x] alternatives
3. **Use-Case Pages (Personas)** — [product] for [audience]
4. **Integrations** — [tool] + [tool]
5. **Templates / Generators** — [type] template, [x] generator
6. **Converters** — [x] to [y]
7. **Examples / Inspiration** — [type] examples
8. **Directories** — [category] tools
9. **Glossary** — what is [term]
10. **Localization** — translated versions of existing pages

Core thesis: instead of pages, think in systems — write a structure once, replicate across hundreds of keyword variations.

Results claimed:

- 692,000 clicks in 4 months
- $1M+ in revenue from search
- 800%+ traffic growth in under 90 days

### LLM SEO case study post (2026-04-13)

A second post reframes SEO around AI answer engines and LLM discovery.

Key claims:

- Client revenue grew from $20K MRR to $100K+ MRR in 4 months
- LLM traffic converts at 18% in the cited study
- SEO now drives 95%+ of the client's organic traffic
- The shared Search Console image shows 171K clicks, 1.25M impressions, 13.7% CTR, and 12.4 average position

Operational details emphasized in the post:

- capture high-intent "AI study tool" keywords
- displace competitors to win 5K+ daily clicks
- rebuild pages for depth and intent
- raise DR from 5 to 21 with backlinks
- layer [[llm-seo]] on top of [[programmatic-seo]]
