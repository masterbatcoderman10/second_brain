# BRIA AI

AI company specializing in legally licensed generative AI models for content creation. Developer of the RMBG ([[rmbg-1-4]] and [[rmbg-2-0]]) family of background removal models — the most widely used open background removal models on HuggingFace.

## Overview

BRIA AI differentiates itself through its emphasis on legally licensed training data and bias mitigation. Their models are trained exclusively on images with proper commercial licenses, making them more suitable for enterprise use cases than models trained on scraped data.

## Key Products

- **RMBG-1.4** — flagship background removal model, 915K monthly downloads, CC BY-NC license
- **RMBG-2.0** — next-generation background removal, improved accuracy, CC BY-NC license
- **Bria API** — cloud-hosted versions of RMBG models with commercial licensing included

## Business Model

BRIA AI offers a tiered model:
1. **HuggingFace (free):** Source-available weights for non-commercial use via CC BY-NC
2. **Commercial license:** Required for business deployment — available via direct agreement
3. **Bria API:** Fully managed cloud API with commercial licensing included

## Relationship to Open Source

BRIA AI positions itself as "source-available" rather than fully open source. The model weights are publicly downloadable, but commercial use requires a paid license. This is distinct from fully open models like [[birefnet]] (MIT) or MLX-community models (Apache 2.0).
