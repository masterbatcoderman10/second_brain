# khairallah-ai-agents-team

X Article by [[khairallah-al-awady]] (@eng_khairallah1); full article (9,861 chars, May 5 2026).

Describes a 3-agent system for solo founders to replace their first 3 hires: research agent (market intelligence), content agent (full content lifecycle with quality gates), and operations agent (email/meetings/reporting). Uses Claude + MCP servers + agentic workflows. Claims 70–80% coverage at a fraction of $180K/year hiring cost.
