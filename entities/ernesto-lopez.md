## AI Influencer Playbook (Summary)

**Core thesis:** Real influencers are "100% cooked" — too expensive, too slow, work with competitors. AI creators replace them at a fraction of the cost.

**The 3-box framework for AI influencer success:**
1. **Painkiller app** — pick an app that solves a real, specific problem
2. **Killer onboarding** — product must convert the traffic once it arrives
3. **Good CTA** — content must drive a clear call to action

**Volume is the algorithm strategy:** Post 10 reels/day, study what goes viral, replicate at speed. Most won't work; the winners drive outsized results.
