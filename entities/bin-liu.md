# Bin Liu

HeyGen engineer who announced the open-sourcing of [[hyperframes]] as the "Agentic Video is HTML: Open Sourcing HyperFrames" post.

## Source

> [Agentic Video is HTML: Open Sourcing HyperFrames](https://x.com/liu8in/status/2044827628700684463)

## About

Bin Liu authored the HeyGen announcement framing HyperFrames as the convergence point between AI agent coding capabilities and video editing — positioning HTML as the agent-native video editing format.

Core thesis: "What the symphony was to Beethoven, play was to Shakespeare — HTML is to agents."

HyperFrames lets Claude Code (or any AI coding agent) generate MP4/MOV/WebM videos by writing HTML/CSS with `data-` attribute annotations, then rendering through a local toolchain.

## Relationship to HyperFrames

Bin is a core contributor to HyperFrames at HeyGen. The announcement credits the full HyperFrames v1.0.0 team: Abhay (@AbhayZala7), Jake (@JakeFromHeyGen), James (@Rames_Jusso), Miguel (@Miguel07Code), and Vance Ingalls.

## Claude Design + HyperFrames Integration

[Another post from Bin Liu](https://x.com/liu8in/status/2045266734450720975) demonstrates a baby from Claude Design + HyperFrames — using Claude Design as the prompt/interface layer on top of HyperFrames for video output. The combination frames Claude Design as the "design editor" and HyperFrames as the video rendering engine.
