# George Clements Paid Ads Agency Funnel

X Article by [[george-clem]] analyzing Daniel Iles's Viral Coach agency funnel generating $40M+/year via paid ads.

## Summary

George Clements reverse-engineered Viral Coach's Meta ads system and documented the full funnel architecture: broad TAM targeting, one high-ticket offer, 30/30/30/10 creative mix, VSL + short-form funnel variants, 3-tier lead routing, and a 3-function setter system for maximizing booking rates.

## Key Details

- **Author**: George Clements (@georgeclem), founder of Paid House Media ($1.5M+ Google Ads agency for ecom) and The Advertising Co
- **Subject**: Daniel Iles's Viral Coach agency ($40M+/yr)
- **Platform**: Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
- **Source**: x.com/georgeclem/status/2051697261101322556

## Core Framework

- Broad TAM with 1 high-ticket offer scales further than narrow TAM with multiple offers
- Messaging consistency across ads, landing page, VSL, and sales call
- 30/30/30/10 creative mix: direct offer / case study / educational / wildcard
- 3-tier lead routing: Tier 1 → senior closers, Tier 2 → junior closers/setters, Tier 3 → email nurture
- 3-function setter system: call partial submits, recover no-shows, confirm bookings
