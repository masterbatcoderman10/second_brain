# Viktor Seraleev

X creator [@seraleev](https://x.com/seraleev). App Store optimization consultant who shared case study data on turning $250/month apps into $5K+ MRR through packaging improvements.

## Source

Referenced in Paul Solt's [$250 to $5K MRR thread](https://x.com/PaulSolt/status/2045580498232373433) as the origin of the app store conversion data. Claim: following his small consistent changes (organic traffic, Reddit posts, comments) on an old app took it from $250-300/month to $5K/month over a year.

## Core Claims

- 12 app store experiments available at super-easy-apps.kit.com
- 75% of his revenue comes from paywall placement before users access the app
- Icon design: bold color, one clear subject, readable at 60×60 App Store search size
- Screenshots should tell a story, not teach the app
- Organic traffic + packaging changes are sufficient (no paid acquisition mentioned)
