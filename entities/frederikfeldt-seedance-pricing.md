# Frederik Feldt: Seedance 2.0 Pricing Analysis

## Overview

@FrederikFeldt (Frederik Feldt) posted a thread analyzing the markup charged by hosted AI video platforms for Seedance 2.0, claiming to offer direct ByteDance API access at a fraction of the cost.

## Key Claims

### Platform pricing comparison

| Platform | Price/second | 10-second gen | Minute (6 gen) | Monthly daily |
|----------|-------------|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| Higgsfield | $0.25 | $2.50 | $15.00 | $450+ |
| YouArt | $0.37 | $3.70 | $22.20 | $660+ |
| Fal AI | $0.25 | $2.50 | $15.00 | $450+ |
| Feldt's platform | ~$0.175 | <$1.75 | <$10.00 | <$200 |
| Raw ByteDance API | $0.10 | $1.00 | $6.00 | $170 |

### Operational claims

- **114M views** generated on a single TikTok account in 28 days using Seedance 2.0
- **65,000 followers** gained in that period
- **4.4M likes** on a single post
- All content on that account was AI-generated

### Access restrictions claimed

- BytePlus (ByteDance's international cloud platform) reportedly pulled Seedance 2.0 API access for US-based users after Hollywood copyright backlash
- Jimeng (ByteDance's consumer product) requires a Chinese phone number and Chinese payment methods
- Danish entity reportedly retains access — Feldt operates through Denmark

## What he's building

A platform offering Seedance 2.0 at 2.5–3.7× cheaper than existing hosted platforms, targeting content creators and AI video operators who use Seedance 2.0 daily for production. Beta opened to 100 people via follow + retweet + "SEEDANCE" comment.

## Substantive thread reply

@AIAffGabriel noted: AI video cost directly affects affiliate margins at scale — "If you're posting 10–20 videos/day across accounts, generation cost becomes a real line item. The difference between $600/month and $150/month is literally more testing budget."

## Evidence level

- All pricing figures are **unverified claims from a single source**
- Operational metrics (views, followers) are **unverified claims from a single source**
- The claims about BytePlus restrictions and jurisdictional access have **not been independently confirmed**
