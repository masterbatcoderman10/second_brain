# Ashwin Gopinath

X creator (@ashwingop) writing about Company Brain — a framework for enterprise AI memory systems.

## Company Brain Series

Authored the "Company Brain" series on X exploring the three-layer memory architecture for enterprise AI:

1. **Part 1**: [Why most companies have data but no memory](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2049641901410955694)
2. **Part 2**: [Factual Memory](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2049885545288077720) — preserving what happened, where the source is, who owns it, when it changed, and how things connect
3. **Part 3**: [Interaction Memory](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2050963469898506342) — preserving what happened *between* people before the artifact existed; the chain of thought of the organization
4. **Part 4**: [Action Memory](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2051317871750558077) — how the company moves; procedural, trigger, execution, and outcome memory; agentic layer
5. **Part 5**: [Memory Is State, Not a Service](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2051691477831745907)
6. **Part 6**: [Lessons From Building A Company Brain](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2052051032566530216)
7. **Part 7**: [Brain Needs Semantic And Ontology](https://x.com/ashwingop/status/2052407955086254262) — Company Brain as infrastructure layer; MCP, markdown brains, AWS analogy; why shared infra with company-specific ontology beats building from scratch
