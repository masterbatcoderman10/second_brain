# Alex Vacca

X creator (@itsalexvacca) and founder of ColdIQ and aiagency.io. Left an $80K job at Sam Altman's company in 2023 to build an AI-powered B2B services agency. Documented the "services-as-software" solo-operator business model in a viral X Article (April 2026).

## Key Claims

- ColdIQ crossed $6.47M annual revenue working with 300+ B2B companies and running 2,000+ campaigns
- Helped 155 agencies launch through the aiagency.io coaching program
- Accelerator has 287 members building AI sales agencies
- The services-as-software model enables one person to generate revenue previously requiring a 30-person agency

## Businesses

- **ColdIQ** — B2B marketing agency; see [[coldiq]] for full entity page
- **aiagency.io** — 12-week accelerator for operators building service-as-a-software businesses; 155 operators launched

## Philosophy

- Distinguishes wealth *creators* (businesses, high-demand technical skills) from wealth *amplifiers* (stock market, crypto, real estate)
- Advocates treating AI agency work as a real business from week one, not a passive-income side project
- Emphasizes outbound precision over volume: "right ICP, right offer, right intent signal, right copy"
- Retainers closed is the only metric that matters; "agents built" and "emails sent" are vanity metrics
