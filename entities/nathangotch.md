# nathangotch

X creator (@nathangotch) focused on SEO, content strategy, and local SEO. Founder of Gotch SEO Academy.

## Overview

Posted "The real definition of SEO (in 2026)" — a framework rebranding SEO as **Search Everywhere Optimization** rather than chasing new acronyms like AI SEO, GEO, or AEO. Argues the local pack is an undervalued commercial traffic source with relatively low barrier to entry compared to blog content.

## Key Framework: Search Everywhere Optimization

Argues that modern SEO means optimizing for multiple surfaces simultaneously:

- **Local pack** — increasingly competitive for commercial queries; correlates with AI Mode, AI Overview, and Google Gemini visibility
- **AI Overview / AI Mode** — Google's AI-powered answer surfaces
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** — optimization for AI answer engines
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — structured content optimized to be extracted as answers

Core thesis: stop chasing acronyms, focus on the underlying principle — being found everywhere buyers search.
