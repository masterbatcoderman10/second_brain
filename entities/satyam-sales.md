# Satyam Sales

X creator (@vizionaryfocuss) who published "how to sell so good competition becomes irrelevant" — a comprehensive B2B sales methodology post.

## Source

> [how to sell so good competition becomes irrelevant](https://x.com/vizionaryfocuss/status/2045144802140750009)

## Key Thesis

**"The company that converts at the highest rate wins. Not the company with the best service. The one with its sales infrastructure dialled in tight enough."**

Core arguments:
- Competing on the wrong things (case studies, brand, price) is a losing game — fight on terrain competitors have already claimed
- The one dimension to overtake established competitors immediately: quality of the sales conversation itself
- Elite closers present a *category of one*, not an offer — comparison mechanism breaks down
- Price is determined by perceived value constructed in the call — market rate is just the floor
- Conviction (not performance) is what sophisticated buyers read

## Sales Infrastructure Framework

- Tight qualification (right prospects on calendar)
- Pre-call nurture (case studies + objection pre-handling)
- Objection libraries (tested language per predictable objection)
- Call reviews with specific feedback
- Metrics that matter: close rate by rep, cash per call, show rate by source, objection frequency

## Credibility

Claims: 50+ companies coached, 170+ sales reps placed, sub-10% → 25-35% close rates achieved.
