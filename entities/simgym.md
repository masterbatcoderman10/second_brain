# simgym

Shopify's A/B testing simulation system. Runs hundreds of simulated shoppers through merchant storefronts to measure conversion impact of changes.

## How It Works

- Each shopper has a persona, budget, and shopping intent
- Runs in real cloud browsers
- Sees the page, decides what to do, clicks, scrolls, adds to cart, or leaves
- Simulated A/B test in minutes instead of weeks

## Inspiration for buy-or-bounce

[[ole-lehmann]] rebuilt the concept as a free Claude skill called [[buy-or-bounce]], which achieves the 80/20 result in ~3 minutes on a single page instead of requiring dedicated AI infrastructure costing millions.
