## AI UGC System

Ollie describes a workflow for creating AI-generated UGC (User-Generated Content) ads:
- Use Nano Banana 2 (FreePik) for images → full control over first frame
- Use Kling for image-to-video → cheap, reliable output
- Optionally Seedance 2.0 for video
- Pinterest/Cosmos for inspiration to recreate in FreePik/HiggsField

**Key insight:** The old Sora method was unreliable (same prompt = different output every time). Current tools allow 100% control over every second.

**Brand attraction strategy:**
- Post content targeted to specific brand ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Use niche terminology and environments matching the brand's audience
- AI UGC character must look like the ICP — relatable over polished

**Pricing signal:** Ollie explicitly avoids the "10k/month" clickbait framing; targets £1-2K/month realistic side income.
